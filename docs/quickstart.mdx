## Get started in 5 minutes

This guide walks you through creating an account, connecting your first data source, and seeing your first verified insight. No technical background required.

<Info>
  **What you'll need:** An email address and a data file (CSV, Excel, or JSON) or database credentials. If you don't have data handy, DataLaser provides sample datasets to explore.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    ### Sign up

    Go to [app.datalaser.io](https://app.datalaser.io) and create your account with email or Google SSO.

    ```
    1. Enter your work email
    2. Set a password (min. 12 characters)
    3. Verify your email via the confirmation link
    ```

    ### Create your first project

    After verifying, you'll be prompted to create a **project**. Projects are containers for related data sources and analyses. Think of them as workspaces for a specific business area.

    <Tip>
      **Naming your project:** Use something descriptive like "Sales Analytics Q1" or "Kundenanalyse 2026". You can always rename it later.
    </Tip>

    Give your project a name and optional description, then continue.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your data">
    ### Upload a file or connect a database

    DataLaser supports two ways to get data in:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Upload a file">
        Drag and drop or click to upload. Supported formats:

        | Format  | Extensions         |
        |---------|--------------------|
        | CSV     | `.csv`             |
        | Excel   | `.xlsx`, `.xls`    |
        | JSON    | `.json`            |
        | Parquet | `.parquet`         |

        DataLaser auto-detects delimiters, encodings (including UTF-8 with BOM, common in German exports), date formats, and decimal separators (comma vs. period).

        <Tip>
          **German Excel exports** often use semicolons as delimiters and commas as decimal separators. DataLaser handles this automatically. No need to reformat your file.
        </Tip>
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Connect a database">
        Choose from **17 supported connectors**:

        - **Databases:** PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQL Server
        - **NoSQL:** MongoDB
        - **Warehouses:** Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, Databricks
        - **Commerce & Payments:** Shopify, Stripe, Square
        - **Advertising:** Google Ads, Meta Ads
        - **Analytics:** Google Analytics
        - **Accounting & Banking:** QuickBooks, Xero, Plaid

        Enter your connection credentials. All credentials are encrypted with **AES-256-GCM** before storage.

        ```
        Host:     your-db.example.com
        Port:     5432
        Database: analytics
        User:     readonly_user
        Password: ••••••••
        ```

        <Warning>
          **Use a read-only database user.** DataLaser only needs SELECT access. We recommend creating a dedicated user with minimal permissions.
        </Warning>
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="See your first insight">
    ### Auto-profiling begins immediately

    The moment your data lands, DataLaser:

    1. **Profiles every column**: types, distributions, missing values, outliers
    2. **Detects applicable templates**: matching your data schema against 42 industry templates
    3. **Runs auto-analyses**: correlations, trends, anomalies, segments, and more

    This typically takes **10-30 seconds** for files under 100 MB.

    ### Your Insights page

    Navigate to the **Insights** tab to see what DataLaser found. Each insight card shows:

    - A plain-language summary of the finding
    - A supporting visualization (chart, distribution, or table)
    - The statistical method used and confidence level
    - An option to explore further in Studio

    <Tip>
      **Not seeing what you expected?** Check the **Health** tab for your data source. A low quality score (below 60) usually means missing values or inconsistent formatting that you can fix at the source.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

Now that you have your first insights, you can:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Explore in Ask Data" icon="comments">
    Ask follow-up questions in natural language. Try: *"What's driving the variance in Q3?"* or *"Welche Kunden haben den h&ouml;chsten Lifetime Value?"*
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deep dive in Studio" icon="flask">
    Open any insight in Studio to customize the analysis, adjust parameters, or chain it with other analyses.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Connect more data" icon="plug">
    Add additional data sources to unlock cross-source analyses, e.g., correlate marketing spend with revenue.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Invite your team" icon="users">
    Share the project with colleagues. They'll see the same insights and can ask their own questions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  **Need help?** Every insight includes an explanation of the methodology. Click "How was this calculated?" on any insight card for a detailed breakdown.
</Note>
